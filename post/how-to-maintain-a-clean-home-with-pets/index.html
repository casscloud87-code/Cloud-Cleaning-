Pet-friendly cleaning

How to maintain a clean home with pets

A pet-friendly home can still feel fresh and guest-ready when cleaning is consistent and focused on the areas where fur, dirt, and odors collect fastest.

Clean floors often

Vacuum and mop traffic paths, feeding areas, and rooms where pets spend the most time. Clean under furniture and along baseboards where fur and dust settle.

Wash bedding and soft surfaces

Pet beds, blankets, throws, and washable covers should be cleaned often. This keeps odors from spreading into bedrooms and living spaces.

Plan recurring help when life is busy

Professional recurring cleaning can keep bathrooms, kitchens, floors, and shared rooms under control while pet owners focus on their schedules.
