Pet-friendly cleaning

How to keep your home clean with pets around

Pets bring comfort and energy into a home, but they also add fur, tracked-in dirt, nose marks, and odors that need a steady cleaning routine.

Control fur before it spreads

Brush pets regularly, vacuum soft surfaces, and clean under furniture where fur tends to gather. Wash pet bedding often so odors do not settle into rooms.

Protect entry areas

Keep a washable mat near doors and wipe paws after walks or yard time. This helps reduce dirt on floors, rugs, sofas, and bedding.

Use product preferences that fit the home

Homes with pets may benefit from fragrance-light or eco-conscious cleaning products by request. Cloud Cleaning Services can work around preferences for pet-friendly routines and sensitive households.
